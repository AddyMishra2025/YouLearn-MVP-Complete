# MoSCoW Prioritisation (MVP)

## Must Have
- **AI-guided path kickoff**: A structured way to capture the learner’s role/skill, goal, and level (modal-based intent capture). 
- **Personalized learning path view**: Core path overview with levels, lesson cards, and clear CTA to start learning. 
- **Lesson consumption experience**: Video player plus AI summary, notes, transcript tabs, and quiz checkpoint. 
- **Progress visibility**: Progress bar and contextual feedback inside the lesson view. 
- **Basic navigation & routing**: Primary routes for home, learning path, resources, lessons, trending, achievements, sign-in, and start.

## Should Have
- **Resource prep step**: Curated resources page before lessons to reduce drop-off and build confidence. 
- **Achievements dashboard**: Milestones, earned badges, and in-progress tracking to reinforce motivation. 
- **Trending discovery**: A curated list of popular tracks to inspire exploration. 
- **Feedback + delight**: Toasts, loading state, and celebratory moments (confetti) to reinforce success.

## Could Have
- **Social sharing flow**: Generated LinkedIn/Twitter share copy for achievements. 
- **Testimonials & social proof**: User quotes and outcomes on the home page. 
- **Newsletter capture**: Email subscription in the footer. 
- **Expanded role presets**: More curated role templates and levels.

## Won’t Have (for MVP)
- **Payments or subscription billing**
- **Real AI recommendation engine** (current implementation is simulated)
- **Full authentication & user management** (current sign-in/up are mock flows)
- **Hiring marketplace, job matching, or credentialing**
- **Mobile native apps**
